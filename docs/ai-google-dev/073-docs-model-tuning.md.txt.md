> [!warning]
> Fine-tuning is **not available** in the Gemini API or AI Studio following deprecation of Gemini 1.5 Flash-001 (May 2025). It remains supported in [Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform](https://cloud.google.com/gemini-enterprise-agent-platform/models/gemini-use-supervised-tuning).

No immediate plans to restore fine-tuning support. Share your use case on the [developer forum](https://discuss.ai.google.dev/c/gemini-api/4).
